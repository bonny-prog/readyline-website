FAQ

What builders ask first.

Who can open a trade account?

Licensed contractors, builders, remodelers, installers, kitchen and bath pros, and property managers. We're wholesale-only — we don't sell retail, and we'll never compete with you for your customer.

What does delivery cost and how fast is it?

Stocked finishes deliver to your jobsite within five business days inside the core radius (Central and Eastern MA and Southern NH), with scheduled weekly routes to Maine and Vermont. Will-call pickup at the Central MA warehouse is always available. Your first delivery inside the core radius is free.

RTA or assembled?

Both. Order flat-pack to save on freight and storage, or have us assemble before delivery so your crew installs straight off the truck. Assembly is priced per cabinet in the builder book.

What happens if a part arrives damaged?

Send your rep a photo. Replacement parts ship within 48 hours — no claim forms, no waiting weeks for a freight inspection. It's the service promise that built the brand.

Do you offer net terms?

Yes. Accounts typically start on payment-at-delivery and move to net terms after 90 days of order history. Multi-family and project orders are quoted with deposit schedules.

Can you handle multi-family projects?

Yes — standard-spec frameless and shaker packages are exactly what multi-family work needs. Project orders are quoted as a package with staged deliveries matched to your construction schedule.
