ReadyLine Euro

RL-EUR · Frameless series. Full-access European construction for modern kitchens and multi-family work — maximum storage per opening, clean flat-panel fronts.

The Build

Frameless, full-access, factory-precise.

ReadyLine Euro is built on a ¾-inch frameless case with screw-and-dowel joinery for a dead-square box. MDF-core PET doors (matte or gloss) and woodgrain melamine fronts hang on heavy-duty concealed soft-close hinges over metal drawer boxes.

  • DoorsMDF-core PET / melamine, laser-edged
  • Drawer boxMetal · soft-close
